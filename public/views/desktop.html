This Is My Story

In 2012 I studied Web Development at The Starter League, an intense programming boot camp. While there I honed the skill-set of a digital craftsman. Skills like HTML5, CSS3, Ruby on Rails, Javascript, and jQuery have allowed me to work on a multitude of different projects designed for various platforms. I am well accustomed to the challenges of Cross-Browser Quirks, Responsive Development, Mobile Development, and International Development. Working as a professional in the web industry these last few years has opened my eyes to a lot, and I'm reminded every day that I'm never done learning. Most recently I've been diving into new exciting javascript technologies such as AngularJS, ExpressJS, and NodeJS. I want to continue to contribute to making the web a better tool for humanity.

Shortly after becoming a professional Developer after my session at The Starter League, I began to fall in love with designing the interfaces I had been coding day in and day out. I started to follow design websites and forums and began to learn everything I could about clean and proper UX Design. I try to draw and code daily while staying involved with communities like Dribbble and Codepen. While working at Keeper Security I was often consulted in the design process and even took the lead design roles on both a website re-design as well as an app re-design. Graphic Design is something I truly enjoy, and while I'm a Developer first and foremost, I still enjoy creating both Graphic and UX Designs.

My Best Work

UI8

UI8 is the asset store operated by the team at Creativedash. During my time there, the senior developer and myself built a brand new node-based e-commerce platform. It was our first node/angular endeavour and I learned a tremendous amount about these exciting new javascript technologies. This site made it to the awwwards where it received an honorable mention.

TAIO CRUZ

While working at Creativedash, Jacob "Taio" Cruz approached the team to re-do his personal site. After getting some hot PSD's from the creative director, I built this site using php, jQuery, and Wordpress. The client was extremely pleased with the result.

PINMSG

Pinmsg is another client I worked with during my time at Creativedash. I really enjoyed this website because of the slick animations and page transitions. This was the first apple-esque controlled scrolling site I worked on, and I had a blast animating each slide. HTML5 videos for the screen content was the icing on the cake.

iOS Apps

iBACC

iBACC was the first iPhone app I ever created. It is a very simple blood alcohol concentration calculator. I learned a tremendous amount about Adobe Phone-Gap and the App-Store process. This app was built using HTML, CSS3, and D3JS. Once running, I packaged it in a Phone-Gap wrapper and it was off to the store.

Cat Napper!

Cat Napper! was my second iPhone app, however it was my first iPhone Game. I built this 2D infinite platformer using Unity Game Engine. Game development was really fun to dive into, and I also really enjoyed learning about apple's iAD program.
